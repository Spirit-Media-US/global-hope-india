## Projects / Programs

### 1. Bibles for India
"1 billion people in India have never held a Bible" (out of 1.4 billion population).
Donors can contribute directly to Bible distribution.
**CTA:** Donate to Bibles for India

### 2. Orphan Care / Child Development
"India is home to half of the world's orphans."
Supporters can sponsor orphan care initiatives.
**CTA:** Sponsor an orphan

### 3. Community / Village Development
"Transforming whole communities through building strong churches and business leaders for Christ."
Village-level initiatives.

### 4. Practical Resources
Support for:
- Clean water projects
- Hygiene kits
- Motorcycles for leaders
- Church buildings and baptisteries
- Disaster relief efforts

### 5. Leadership Development
Training programs for emerging Christian leaders — church planters, pastors, worship leaders.

### 6. Church Planting
Direct funding for establishing new congregations across India.

### 7. Business Development
Supporting "The Shepherd's House" and entrepreneurship initiatives.

### 8. North East Healthcare Fund
Regional healthcare access program serving Northeast India.
