## Privacy Policy

**Global Hope India (GHI) values its users' privacy.** This Privacy Policy explains how the organization collects and uses personal information from website visitors and online service users.

GHI designed this policy to "ensure those affiliated with Global Hope India of our commitment and realization of our obligation not only to meet, but to exceed, most existing privacy standards."

The organization reserves the right to modify this policy at any time. Users are advised to review it frequently for updates. If GHI plans to use personal information differently than originally stated, users will receive email notification and may opt out.

---

### Information Collection

**Types of information collected:**
- Voluntarily provided data (name, address, email, billing/credit card information)
- Automatically collected data (cookies, tracking technologies, server logs)
- Non-personal demographic information (age, gender, income, browser type, IP address)

**Collection occurs through:** surveys, membership forms, and emails that users knowingly complete.

---

### Collection Purposes & Retention

GHI collects data to:
- Better understand user needs
- Improve services and products
- Send promotional emails (with consent)
- Request survey participation (with consent)
- Customize website experiences

"The data we collect from you will be stored for no longer than necessary," based on relevance, legal obligations, and legitimate interests.

---

### Information Disclosure

GHI may disclose information when:
- Providing requested services/products
- Required by law or subpoena
- Sharing with outside auditors under confidentiality agreements
- Enforcing Terms of Service
- Protecting organizational rights and property

Aggregate data may be shared in ways that prevent identity identification.

---

### Non-Marketing Communications

GHI reserves the right to contact users for non-marketing purposes, including security breaches, account issues, and service changes.

---

### Children Under 13

"Global Hope India's website is not directed to, and does not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from, children under the age of thirteen (13)."

---

### Unsubscribe/Opt-Out

Users may discontinue communications by emailing **Info@globalhopeindia.org** with unsubscribe requests.

### External Links

GHI is not responsible for other websites' privacy policies.

### EU Users Notice

GHI operations are primarily US-based. Information transferred to the US from the EU is protected under the EU-US Privacy Shield framework.

### Security Measures

GHI implements:
- Encryption for sensitive information (credit card data)
- HTTPS protocol verification
- Employee access restrictions
- Secure offline storage
- SSL authentication
- TRUSTe licensure
- VeriSign security
