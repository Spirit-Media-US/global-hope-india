**H2:** "Empowering Radical Transformation"

## How GHI Began (History)

**H2:** "How GHI Began"

The founder's first India trip occurred in 1998, when "God planted a part of his heart in India." He returned three years later with his 11-year-old son, then again three years after with his 11-year-old daughter.

GHI incorporated as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization in 2003 "for the sole purpose of having a bank account and the ability to offer donors a tax-deductible receipt."

Initial operations grew from one annual team to two teams yearly, then three. In October 2010, while speaking to gospel workers in Hyderabad, the founder's heart was "broken for the pastors of India," leading to his full-time commitment to empowering Indian churches.

"Thousands of Americans and Indians have been radically transformed through this mission, and millions of dollars have been collected for the Lord's work through Global Hope India."

## Statement of Faith

**H2:** "Statement of Faith"

### God the Father
"We believe in God the Father, Creator of everything, who loves the world he created and sent his only Son to be our Savior."

### God the Son (Jesus)
"We believe that God the Son is Jesus of Nazareth, the Savior sent by God, Israel's Messiah, born of a virgin, fully man and fully God."

### God the Holy Spirit
"We believe that God the Holy Spirit quickens us from death to life, indwells all those who receive Jesus Christ by faith as Lord and Savior and continuously conforms believers to the image of Jesus Christ."

### Human Nature and Sin
"We believe that God created all men in his image, to be in perfect fellowship with him, but that this fellowship was broken by sin. Subsequently, all men are born sinners and are in need of salvation. Our relationship with God can only be restored through repentance of sin and belief in Jesus Christ for salvation."

### Atonement
"We believe the death of Jesus Christ on the cross is sufficient in itself to make atonement for the sin of the world – an atonement available to all who repent of their sins and believe in him alone for forgiveness and salvation."

### Resurrection
"We believe Jesus Christ died on the cross, was buried, and the third day he bodily arose from the dead guaranteeing the bodily resurrection of all those who have received eternal life through faith in him."

### Salvation by Grace
"We believe salvation from judgment, forgiveness of sin and eternal life are free gifts of God's grace and cannot be earned, deserved, inherited or bought."

### Eternal Judgment
"We believe only those who believe in Jesus Christ and receive him into their inner being will escape eternal judgment and be accepted into heaven."

### Second Coming
"We believe in the imminent, visible, bodily return of Jesus Christ to this earth to reign in righteousness and justice."

### The Church
"We believe the true Church is not an organization, but the body of Christ made up of those who have personally trusted Jesus Christ as Savior and publicly confessed him as Lord."

### Bible Authority
"We believe that the Bible is the Word of God, inspired by the Spirit of God, without error and thus the supreme and final authority for faith and practice."

### Spiritual Disciplines
"We believe that daily Bible reading and prayer are as necessary for the sustenance of spiritual life as food and water are necessary for physical life."

### Great Commission
"We believe that all believers are commanded to be witnesses for Christ, sharing the Gospel to the whole world."
