# Global Hope India -- Content Management Guide

> This guide is for GHI staff who will add and edit blog posts on the website.

---

## Accessing Sanity Studio

1. Go to **https://globalhopeindia.org/studio/** (or the Netlify preview URL until domain is connected)
2. Sign in with the Google account Kevin has authorized
3. You will see the Sanity Studio dashboard with "Blog Posts" in the left sidebar

---

## Adding a New Blog Post

1. Click **Blog Posts** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **+ Create** button (top-right)
3. Fill in the following fields:

| Field | What to Enter | Required? |
|-------|--------------|-----------|
| **Title** | The blog post headline | Yes |
| **Slug** | Click "Generate" -- it creates a URL-friendly version of the title automatically | Yes |
| **Excerpt** | A 1-2 sentence summary (max 200 characters). This appears on the blog listing page and in search results | No, but recommended |
| **Hero Image** | Upload a featured image (drag and drop or click to browse). Recommended size: 1640x924px or similar 16:9 ratio. Add alt text describing the image | No, but recommended |
| **Author** | Defaults to "Global Hope India" -- change if a specific person wrote it | No |
| **Publish Date** | The date the post should show. Use the date picker | Yes |
| **Tags** | Select one or more categories: Partners, Projects, Healthcare, Community, Education | No, but recommended for filtering |
| **Body** | Write the post content here. You can use the toolbar for formatting (see below) | No |
| **SEO Title** | Custom title for Google search results (50-60 characters ideal). If left blank, the regular title is used | No |
| **SEO Description** | Custom description for Google search results (150-160 characters ideal). If left blank, the excerpt is used | No |

4. Click **Publish** (bottom-right) when ready

---

## Writing & Formatting the Body

The body editor works like a simple word processor:

- **Bold** -- Select text, click the **B** button
- **Italic** -- Select text, click the *I* button
- **Heading 2** -- For major sections within the post. Select the text, then choose "Heading 2" from the style dropdown
- **Heading 3** -- For sub-sections. Choose "Heading 3" from the style dropdown
- **Links** -- Select text, click the link icon, paste the URL

Tip: Keep paragraphs short (2-4 sentences) for easy reading on mobile.

---

## Uploading Images

- **Hero Image**: Click the image field, then drag and drop or click "Upload" to select a file
- **Recommended format**: .webp or .jpg (Sanity optimizes images automatically)
- **Always add Alt Text**: Describe the image in a few words (e.g., "Children at the medical camp in Manipur"). This helps with accessibility and SEO
- **Hotspot**: After uploading, you can click and drag the hotspot circle to choose which part of the image stays visible when cropped

---

## Editing an Existing Post

1. Click **Blog Posts** in the sidebar
2. Find the post in the list (most recent first)
3. Click on it to open the editor
4. Make your changes
5. Click **Publish** to save

---

## Deleting a Post

1. Open the post
2. Click the three-dot menu (top-right of the editor)
3. Select **Delete**
4. Confirm the deletion

Note: Deleted posts are removed from the website after the next site rebuild (usually within a few minutes).

---

## How Publishing Works

- When you publish or update a blog post, the website **automatically rebuilds** within a few minutes
- You do not need to contact Spirit Media to push changes live
- New posts appear on the Blog page, the Impact page (latest posts section), and the home page (if among the 6 most recent)
- Posts are searchable by title, excerpt, author, and tags

## Tag Categories Explained

| Tag | Use For |
|-----|---------|
| **Partners** | Stories about ministry partners in India |
| **Projects** | Updates on specific funded projects |
| **Healthcare** | Medical camps, health initiatives |
| **Community** | Community development, special needs, outreach |
| **Education** | Schools, scholarships, training programs |

You can assign multiple tags to a single post. Visitors can filter by tag on the blog page.

---

## Tips for Great Blog Posts

1. **Start with a compelling title** -- Keep it under 70 characters
2. **Write a strong excerpt** -- This is what people see first on the blog listing page
3. **Use a high-quality hero image** -- It's the first thing visitors notice
4. **Break up long posts** with Heading 2 and Heading 3 sections
5. **Include a call to action** -- The site automatically shows a donation CTA below every post, but you can also link to specific projects or the Get Involved page in your body text
6. **Fill in SEO fields** -- Even a basic SEO title and description helps your post appear in Google search results
